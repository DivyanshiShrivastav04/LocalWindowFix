Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common window service questions

How long does window installation typically take?

+

Most residential window installations take 1-2 days depending on the number of windows. Commercial projects may take longer based on scope and complexity.

Do you offer emergency window repair services?

+

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency window repair services for urgent situations like broken glass, security concerns, or weather damage.

What warranty do you provide on your work?

+

We offer a comprehensive 10-year warranty on all window installations and a 2-year warranty on repair services. Manufacturer warranties also apply.

Are you licensed and insured?

+

Yes, we are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. Our certifications are current and we carry comprehensive liability and workers' compensation insurance.

Can you help me choose the right windows for my property?

+

Absolutely! Our experts will assess your property, discuss your needs and budget, and recommend the best window solutions for energy efficiency, aesthetics, and functionality.
