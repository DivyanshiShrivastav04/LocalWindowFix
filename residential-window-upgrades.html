Window Styles for Every Home

  • Double Hung Windows: Classic style with top and bottom sashes that slide up and down
  • Casement Windows: Side-hinged windows that open outward for maximum ventilation
  • Sliding Windows: Horizontal sliding operation perfect for contemporary homes
  • Bay & Bow Windows: Projecting windows that add space and architectural interest
  • Picture Windows: Large fixed windows for unobstructed views and natural light
  • Awning Windows: Bottom-hinged windows ideal for ventilation even during rain

Window Upgrade FAQ

Common questions about residential window upgrades

How much can I save on energy bills with new windows?

+

Most homeowners see 10-25% reduction in heating and cooling costs with energy-efficient window upgrades, often paying for themselves over time.

Do window upgrades increase home value?

+

Yes, window upgrades typically increase home value by 5-10% and are highly attractive to potential buyers for their energy efficiency and curb appeal.

Are there tax credits available for window upgrades?

+

Yes, ENERGY STAR certified windows may qualify for federal tax credits and local utility rebates. We'll help you understand available incentives.

How long do window upgrades take to complete?

+

Most residential upgrades take 1-3 days depending on the number of windows. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your home life.
