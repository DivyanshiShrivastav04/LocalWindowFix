Professional Window Frame Replacement Services

When window frames become damaged, warped, or deteriorated beyond repair, complete frame replacement is the best solution. Our expert frame replacement services restore your windows' structural integrity, improve energy efficiency, and enhance your property's appearance.

Signs You Need Frame Replacement

Severe Damage

Rotting wood, rusted metal, or cracked frames beyond repair.

Energy Loss

Drafts, air leaks, and poor insulation around window frames.

Operation Issues

Windows that stick, won't open/close, or have alignment problems.

Aesthetic Concerns

Outdated styles that don't match your home's current look.

Frame Materials We Install

Vinyl Frames

  • Energy-efficient and low maintenance
  • Available in multiple colors
  • Excellent insulation properties
  • Most cost-effective option

Wood Frames

  • Classic, traditional appearance
  • Can be painted or stained
  • Excellent insulation when maintained

Aluminum Frames

  • Durable and long-lasting
  • Slim profiles for modern look
  • Suitable for large windows
  • Low maintenance requirements

Fiberglass Frames

  • Superior durability and strength
  • Excellent thermal performance
  • Can mimic wood appearance
  • Minimal expansion/contraction

Complete Replacement Process

  1. Assessment & Measurement: Thorough evaluation of existing frames and precise measurements
  2. Material Selection: Choose frame material and style that matches your needs and budget
  3. Custom Manufacturing: Frames manufactured to exact specifications for perfect fit
  4. Professional Removal: Careful removal of old frames and preparation of openings
  5. Installation: Expert installation ensuring proper fit, seal, and operation
  6. Finishing Work: Trim installation, caulking, and final adjustments
  7. Quality Testing: Comprehensive testing of operation and energy efficiency

Benefits of Frame Replacement

  • Improved Energy Efficiency: New frames eliminate air leaks and improve insulation
  • Enhanced Durability: Modern materials resist weather, pests, and deterioration
  • Better Operation: Smooth opening and closing with modern hardware
  • Increased Home Value: New window frames boost curb appeal and property value
  • Reduced Maintenance: Modern frames require minimal upkeep
  • Improved Security: Stronger frames and modern locking systems
  • Noise Reduction: Better sealing reduces outside noise

Frame vs. Glass Replacement

When to replace just the glass: Minor glass damage, functioning frames, budget constraints

When to replace the entire frame: Frame damage, poor energy efficiency, operation problems, major renovations

Our experts will assess your situation and recommend the most cost-effective solution.

Frame Replacement FAQ

Common questions about window frame replacement services

How do I know if I need frame replacement or just glass replacement?

+

If frames are rotting, warped, difficult to operate, or have significant air leaks, full replacement is recommended. We provide free assessments to determine the best solution.

How long does frame replacement take?

+

Most residential frame replacements take 1-2 days per window. Custom frames may require 2-4 weeks for manufacturing before installation.

Will frame replacement improve my home's energy efficiency?

+

Yes, new frames with proper insulation and sealing can significantly reduce energy loss and lower heating/cooling costs by 10-25%.

What warranty do you provide on frame replacement?

+

We provide a 10-year warranty on installation workmanship plus manufacturer warranties on frame materials, typically 10-20 years depending on material.
