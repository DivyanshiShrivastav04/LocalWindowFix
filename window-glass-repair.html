Our Glass Repair Process

  1. Assessment: Thorough evaluation of damage and repair options
  2. Measurement: Precise measurements for perfect glass fit
  3. Glass Selection: Choose appropriate glass type and thickness
  4. Safe Removal: Careful removal of damaged glass and old glazing
  5. Installation: Professional installation with proper sealing
  6. Testing: Quality check to ensure proper fit and function

Glass Types We Work With

  • Single Pane Glass: Standard residential and commercial windows
  • Double Pane (Insulated): Energy-efficient dual-pane units
  • Tempered Glass: Safety glass for doors and large windows
  • Laminated Glass: Security and storm-resistant glass
  • Low-E Glass: Energy-efficient coated glass
  • Specialty Glass: Decorative, frosted, and custom glass

When to Repair vs Replace

Repair When:

  • Small cracks or chips
  • Minor scratches
  • Single pane damage
  • Cost-effective solution

Replace When:

  • Extensive damage
  • Multiple cracks
  • Seal failure in double pane
  • Safety glass damage

Glass Repair FAQ

Common questions about our glass repair services

Can all types of glass damage be repaired?

+

Small cracks and chips can often be repaired, but extensive damage, safety glass breaks, or seal failures in double pane windows typically require replacement.

How quickly can you repair my window?

+

We offer same-day service for emergency repairs and common glass sizes. Custom glass may take 1-3 days depending on specifications.

Do you warranty your glass repair work?

+

Yes, we provide a 2-year warranty on all glass repair and replacement work, covering both materials and workmanship.

Can you match the existing glass in my windows?

+

Yes, we can match glass thickness, tint, and special coatings to ensure your repaired window matches the existing windows perfectly.
